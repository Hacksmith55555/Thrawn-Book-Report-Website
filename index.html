Thrawn - Star Wars

Welcome to the Star Wars universe. Explore the captivating story of Grand Admiral Thrawn and his journey through the galaxy.

Main Characters

Grand Admiral Thrawn

Description: Brilliant tactician with a keen understanding of art and culture.

Relationship: Master strategist leading the Imperial forces.

Development: Witness the evolution of Thrawn's character and strategies.

Eli Vanto

Description: Loyal aide with a background in languages.

Relationship: Supportive ally of Thrawn and other characters.

Development: Experience Eli's personal growth and adaptation to new challenges.

Arihnda Pryce

Description: Ambitious and resourceful, striving for power within the Empire.

Relationship: Explore Arihnda's connections with key characters.

Development: Navigate through the hurdles and transformations in Arihnda's journey.

Two Reviews

Author: Star Wars Enthusiast

Date: Jan 1, 2023

Thoughts: An epic journey through the galaxy with Thrawn's genius at the forefront. A must-read for Star Wars fans.

Author: Kelvin Scott

Date: March 1, 2023

Fantastic read. Honestly cannot recommend enough. It’s a real page turner from beginning to end. I could not put it down while reading the book. It has a very steady plot, engaging story, amazing characterisation and it makes you question the morality of the story throughout. You are also hooked throughout with the mystery to the identity of the main antagonist known as Nightswan. It is also fun seeing the growth of the relationship of Thrawn and Eli, acting as the Sherlock homes and John Watson of the Novel. Nightswan acting as the thorn in Thrawns side, his Moriarty of sorts. There are many emotional moments through the course of the story, as we experience thrawns journey, facing the Racism, prejudice and corruption within the Empire left over from the Clone wars. This book also links very well into the established canon of Star Wars Rebels too. Overall I give the Book a 9/10 rating. A must read if you haven’t already. Xx

Author: Imperial Cadet

Date: Dec 22, 2022

Absolutely amazing book. I could not ask for a better origin story for Thrawn than this. The character development in this book is top notch. For anyone who is interested in Star Wars beyond just the movies, do yourself a favor and pick this book up.
